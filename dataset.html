Methodology

A landmark qualifies if it is (1) a real, documented Solana ecosystem event or culture moment, (2) sourceable to a primary or strongly authoritative reference, and (3) memorable enough to teach. Security and outage events lead with official post-mortems, court filings, or Chainalysis; meme/culture entries are documented neutrally as culture, never as investment advice. References are historical citations, not endorsements.
