DEGEN LAUNCHPAD

2024 · MEME · SOURCED

Launch a token in 30 seconds for almost nothing. Solana's meme floodgates never closed again.

What happened

Pump.fun made token creation fast, permissionless, and nearly free, turning meme-coin launches into a constant, high-volume cultural activity on Solana.

Why Solana remembers it

A major product and culture moment — with real speculation and risk attached. It is history, treated neutrally, not an endorsement.
