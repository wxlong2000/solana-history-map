Your Solana history footprint

Paste a Solana wallet address — we read the public chain and show which moments on the map your wallet lived through. Read-only. No signing, no access to your funds, and we don't store your address.

How this works: we call read-only Solana RPC (getParsedTokenAccountsByOwner for current holdings, getSignaturesForAddress for first visible activity). We can only see what you currently hold and the oldest activity the RPC retains — so we say "currently holds", not "claimed the airdrop", and "first activity we can see", not a guaranteed birth date. Your address is never stored and we never request a signature or transaction.
